What can you do with
Crisp Care

Profile and View Reviews

You can view the profile of a potential housekeeper, see the services that they offer, their overall performance rating and what people think of them.

Booking

You can book a housekeeper to provides services by specifying the services you wish to be rendered to you, the date and time frame by which you would like the housekeeper to start and finish rendering the service.

Feedback, Review & Rating

After a service has been rendered, you can leave a feedback on the housekeeper's performance to enable other homeowners make better decisions.

About Us: Making Time for What Matters

We created this web app because juggling a busy life with a clean home can feel impossible. The inspiration struck when Ngozi found herself constantly stressed about maintaining a clean apartment while working long hours. The dream of a sparkling home after a long day felt out of reach.

This was in May, 2024 and the idea for a user-friendly platform to connect with reliable housekeepers was born! We, a team of aspiring developers at Holberton School (check out our portfolio project! Crisp Care ), decided to turn this frustration into a solution.
